CALL FOR PARTICIPATION
Doctoral Colloquium/ Ph.D. Forum
November 16-19, 2020, Yokohama, Japan
Doctoral Colloquium/ Ph.D. Forum
The 18th ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems (SenSys 2020)

The SenSys 2020 Doctoral Colloquium provides an opportunity for doctoral students to explore and develop their research interests in an interdisciplinary workshop, under the guidance of a panel of distinguished researchers.The Forum provides an excellent opportunity for Ph.D. students to present their dissertation research, including work in progress, to the wider embedded networked sensor systems community. This meeting offers an opportunity to share experiences with other Ph.D. students and meet with accomplished international researchers and obtain feedback from them.SenSys proceedings include full papers and Ph.D Forum (Doctoral colloquium) abstracts and workshop papers are also put in the adjunct proceedings.
